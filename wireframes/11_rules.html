Fraud flag rules
🛡️
Flag provider upcoding patterns
Provider patternFraudSupervisor
Active
If the same ICD-CPT procedure combination has been billed by the same provider (NPI) more than 3 times in any 30-day window across different members, flag all new claims from that provider as potential upcoding fraud and route immediately to a supervisor for investigation. Do not auto-approve any claims from this provider until the supervisor clears the flag.

Recommend denial for missing pre-authorization
Pre-authDenialHuman confirms
Active
If the procedure requires pre-authorization per the plan's benefit schedule AND no valid pre-authorization record is found in the system for this member, provider and procedure date AND the billed amount exceeds $1,000, recommend denial with the reason "Pre-authorization required but not on file."
